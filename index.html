Our Story

Meet Saurabh & Divya

Welcome to Samdiv's Chocolate Bowl! The name "Samdiv" is a blend of our names—Saurabh Pardeshi and Divya Kashid. We are a passionate couple who share a deep, unwavering love for all things chocolate.

What started as a shared hobby in our kitchen quickly blossomed into a dream to bring premium, mouth-watering desserts to our beloved city. We wanted to create a cozy spot right here in Junnar where friends, families, and couples could create sweet memories.

Every chocolate bowl, brownie, and shake is handcrafted with the finest ingredients and a whole lot of love. We believe that dessert isn't just food; it's an experience.

"Made for each other, making desserts for you."
